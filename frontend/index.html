About Tharlam Manjushree

Welcome to Tharlam Manjushree, where education meets innovation. Our Student Management System helps teachers and administrators manage student information efficiently and focus more on learning than paperwork.

Our Mission

At Tharlam Manjushree, our mission is to provide a holistic education while using modern technology to simplify administrative tasks. We aim to create an organized and transparent system for students, teachers, and staff.

Features of Our System

  • Store and update student personal and academic details
  • Manage teacher information including subjects taught
  • Search, filter, and track student progress easily
  • Generate reports and maintain organized records

Why Choose Tharlam Manjushree

Our system ensures that all student and teacher data is organized and easily accessible. It helps reduce errors, save time, and allows teachers to focus on teaching while keeping parents informed about their child’s progress.

Help - Tharlam Manjushree

Welcome to the Help Page for the Tharlam Manjushree Student Management System. Here you can find guidance on how to use the system efficiently and troubleshoot common issues.

Getting Started

  • Log in with your provided credentials.
  • Navigate to the Students or Teachers section using the menu.
  • Use the Add button to create new entries for students or teachers.
  • Search and filter data using the search bar at the top of the page.

Managing Data

  • Click on a student or teacher entry to view detailed information.
  • Use the Edit/Update button to modify information.
  • Use the Delete button to remove an entry from the system.

Troubleshooting

  • If you cannot log in, check your username and password.
  • Make sure your internet connection is stable when adding or updating data.
  • If data is not showing, refresh the page or check if the server is running.
  • Contact the system administrator if the problem persists.
